About the Game

Epic Match takes the classic match-3 formula and infuses it with magical energy, powerful characters, and endless strategy.
In this visually stunning game, players embark on an epic journey through enchanted worlds, solving puzzles, unlocking unique heroes, and mastering legendary boosters. Whether you’re a casual player or a match-3 master, you’ll be hooked from your first swap.

Features

  • Unique Heroes

    Summon magical allies with game-changing abilities.

  • Match-3 Evolved

    Combos, chains, and powerful mechanics keep it fresh.

  • Multiple Realms

    Explore fantasy worlds from icy mountains to fire caverns.

  • Daily Rewards

    Login bonuses, treasure chests, and events.

  • Challenging Puzzles

    Strategy matters: plan your moves for maximum impact.

  • Offline Gameplay

    Enjoy anywhere, no internet needed.

  • Free to Play

    Dive in without paying a coin.

How to Play

  • Match 3 or more gems of the same color.

  • Match 4+ tiles in fun shapes to trigger specials.

  • Activate your heroes' abilities when fully charged.

  • Beat the objectives before you run out of moves.
